How to Use Papyromancy

How to Use Our Currency Conversion:

  • First, enter the amount of US currency you wish to convert.

  • Second, select the currency you wish to convert to.

  • Finally, Press the "Convert" button to display information below.

Note that the number displayed is an estimate, not an exact figure of what you'll recieve

What is Purchasing Power Parity?

Purchasing Power Parity is designed to let you know how far your money can take you. Once you press "Convert", you'll find average prices of everyday items and services in USD. Each price listed is an approximation of how much each item/service would cost were you to pay with USD. This is meant to give you an idea of how cheap or expensive staying in your destination country will be.
